About Me

Hello! I'm Volodymyr Komarov, a game programmer based in Canada. My journey from engineering to game development has been fueled by a relentless passion for creating immersive gaming experiences. With Master degree in engineering and degree in Gaming Experience Development from New Brunswick Community College,

I've honed my skills in C++, Java, C#, and JavaScript, alongside mastering game development frameworks. At Digital Extremes, I've immersed myself in the gaming industry, handling complex customer support queries and contributing to community engagement, which has deepened my understanding of what makes games truly engaging.

Game programming for me is the perfect blend of creativity, technology, and storytelling. It's about bringing imaginative worlds to life and crafting experiences that resonate with players globally. I'm excited to explore new technologies, push creative boundaries, and contribute to innovative gaming projects. Check out my portfolio to see the adventures I've created. My Portfolio

Zombie Outlast

"Zombie Outlast" is a thrilling survival game set in a post-apocalyptic world filled with zombies. Developed with SFML and C++, it challenges players to outlive the undead through strategic exploration, resource management, and combat. The game features a responsive world view, advanced animation for realistic movements, and a smart zombie AI that keeps players on their toes. With its engaging mechanics and compelling narrative, "Zombie Outlast" offers an immersive experience in the fight for survival against all odds.

Base Conquest: Battle Connections

"Base Conquest: Battle Connections" is a captivating, low-poly strategy game set in a cartoonish military world. Players engage in tactical base expansion and resource management, focusing on creating efficient connections for success. The game combines simple yet strategic gameplay with a charming art style, offering a light-hearted take on army-themed adventures. Ideal for both strategy enthusiasts and casual gamers, it provides an entertaining mix of base building and lively tactical battles.

"Buried Horrors"

In "Buried Horrors", you find yourself trapped in an abandoned and zombie-infested mine with no weapons. Your only hope for survival is to scavenge for gas to start an old generator that will power an elevator and take you to safety. But be warned, the mine is filled with dangers at every turn and you must use your wits and quick reflexes to avoid becoming a meal for the undead. Can you gather enough gas, start the generator, and make it out alive? The clock is ticking, and the horrors of the mine are closing in.

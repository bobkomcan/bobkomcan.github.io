About Me

Hello! I'm Volodymyr Komarov, but my friend call me Bob. I am a game programmer based in Canada. My journey from engineering to game development has been fueled by a passion for creating immersive games. With Master degree in engineering and degree in Gaming Experience Development from New Brunswick Community College, I've honed my skills in C++, Java, C#, and JavaScript, alongside mastering game development frameworks.

I have 4 years of experience working in Community Team of one of the best Canadian gamedev studios Digital Extremes, I've immersed myself in the gaming industry, handling complex customer support requests and contributing to community engagement, which has deepened my understanding of what makes games truly engaging.

My programming journey started during the pandemic in 2020. Game programming for me is the perfect blend of creativity, technology, and storytelling. It's about bringing imaginative worlds to life and crafting experiences that resonate with players globally. I'm excited to explore new technologies, push creative boundaries, and contribute to innovative gaming projects. Check out my portfolio to see the adventures I've created. Let's make something amazing together.

"Join and Tank"

"Join and Tank" is an action-packed 3D tank game set in the tumultuous times of World War II. In this game, players take on... the role of an American tank crew tasked with a daring assault on entrenched German forces. Your mission is not only to navigate the treacherous battlefield, laden with deadly mines, but also to come to the rescue of your infantry allies. These soldiers have been pinned down by relentless machine gun fire and are in desperate need of support. As a player, you must expertly maneuver your tank to avoid explosives while racing to pick up the stranded infantrymen. Each soldier you save not only boosts your ranks but also brings you one step closer to completing the level. Your ultimate goal is to gather enough of your comrades and deliver them safely to the sanctuary of a bunker, ensuring their survival and your victory. "Join and Tank" blends the thrill of a runner game with strategic elements that require quick thinking and sharp reflexes. Whether dodging hazards or plotting the safest route to rescue your fellow troops, each level promises intense moments and gripping gameplay. Get ready to join the fray and tank your way through history!
